Sensor Data

WeRadiateNY is a startup dedicated to maximizing energy output from small-scale composting units. While the long-term goal is to use compost for heating units, the current priority is to understand the factors affecting composting efficiency. To this end, WeRadiate has created temperature sensors to be placed within compost to monitor conditions in real-time. By experimenting with placement of sensors, size of composting units, and composition of the compost over time, WeRadiate can identify and monitor the key factors for effective composting.
